    IIIT Kota


    FOUNDATION

    IIIT Kota is a joint venture of the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India with industries in Public-Private Partnership model. It is a technical Institute focused on IT sector development with two branches (CSE, ECE) and sophisticated, industrial research oriented curriculum.

    STUDENT LIFE

    The students have started several clubs under the mentorship of MNIT Jaipur and are learning a lot of skills from their IIITK seniors. All the students are actively participating in the Technical and cultural activities. FlairFeasta the annual Techno cultural fest, E-Cell MNIT, Incognito DC, ARC RC are some activities in which the students are deeply involved and developing their skills.
